My small biography

My name is Tyler. I was born in Covington a while ago. I've lived in Memphis since I graduated high school all the way back in 2007. I bumped into things for a long time until I met my wife. We eventually had a boy child. Oh, and we're about to have boy child number 2.

Education History

As for my education history, that is a tricky, winding, unfinished, ADHD riddled timeline.
  • I graduated high school in 2007
  • I started a degree in international business and foreign language before I realized I hated everything about what I just typed.
  • I dropped out two successful semesters later to work in management at UPS before realizing I would wake up 60 years old having completely wasted my life
  • I came back to University of Memphis when I was 23 to finish a double major in Philosophy and sociology. I didn't finish either.
  • Six years later I started taking classes in Computer Science for fun and here I am.
